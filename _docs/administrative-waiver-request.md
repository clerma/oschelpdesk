Use this form to submit a waiver request for events, activities, or ministry programs that require participant acknowledgment of risk or liability. All waivers must be approved before the event takes place.

> **Important:** Waiver requests should be submitted at least **1 week** before your event. Events involving minors, physical activity, or off-campus locations may require additional review.

---

## Submit a Waiver Request

[Submit Waiver Request](https://www.cognitoforms.com){:target="_blank" .action_btn}

---

## When a Waiver is Required

A waiver may be required for activities including:

- Off-campus trips or retreats
- Physical activities (sports, ropes courses, etc.)
- Events involving minors without a parent or guardian present
- Any activity where there is an elevated risk of injury
- Third-party vendor or contractor involvement

---

## What to Include

- Event name, date, and location
- Ministry or department organizing the event
- Description of the activity
- Estimated number of participants
- Whether minors are involved

---

## Questions?

Contact the Administrative team or your campus pastor for guidance on whether your event requires a waiver.
