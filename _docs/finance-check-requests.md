Use the appropriate campus check request form below to submit requests for reimbursements, vendor payments, or ministry purchases. Select your campus and complete the form through Cognito Forms.

> **Note:** All check requests should be submitted at least **5 business days** before payment is needed. Contact the finance team if you have questions.

## What to Include

When completing your check request, have the following ready:

- Purpose of the expense
- Vendor name and payment details
- Dollar amount
- Budget line or ministry name
- Supporting receipts or invoices (attach as PDF or photo)
