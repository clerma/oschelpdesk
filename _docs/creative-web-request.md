Use this form to submit a web development request to the OSC Creative team. This includes new pages, content updates, event listings, landing pages, and other changes to OSC web properties.

> **Note:** Requests are reviewed weekly. Urgent changes (broken links, outdated event info) can be flagged in the form and will be prioritized accordingly.

---

## Submit a Web Request

[Submit Web Development Request](https://www.cognitoforms.com){:target="_blank" .action_btn}

---

## What to Include

- Page or section affected
- Description of the change or new content needed
- Deadline (if applicable)
- Copy/text to be published (proofread before submitting)
- Images or assets to upload (JPG/PNG, 72dpi minimum for web)
- Links to reference pages or examples (optional)

---

## Content Guidelines

- All copy must be final and approved before submission
- Images should be high resolution and properly licensed
- New pages require approval from ministry leadership before going live

---

## Questions?

Contact the Creative team or your campus digital coordinator for assistance.
