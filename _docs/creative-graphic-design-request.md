Use this form to submit a graphic design project request to the OSC Creative team. This includes print materials, social media graphics, event flyers, banners, signage, and other visual assets.

> **Plan ahead:** Standard design requests require a **minimum of 2 weeks** lead time. Rush requests may not be accommodated.

---

## Submit a Graphic Design Request

[Submit Graphic Design Request](https://www.cognitoforms.com){:target="_blank" .action_btn}

---

## What to Include

Before submitting, have the following details ready:

- Project name and ministry/department
- Description of what is needed
- Dimensions or format (print size, social media platform, etc.)
- Event date or deadline
- Copy/text to be included
- Reference images or inspiration (optional)
- Approved budget if printing is required

---

## Tips for a Smooth Process

- The more detail you provide, the faster the team can turn around your request
- Proofread all copy before submitting — revisions after approval may delay delivery
- All designs must align with OSC brand standards

---

## Questions?

Contact the Creative team for project status or to discuss your needs before submitting.
