Use this form to request video production support from the OSC Creative team. This includes sermon bumpers, ministry highlight videos, event recaps, social media reels, and other video content.

> **Plan ahead:** Video projects typically require **3–4 weeks** minimum depending on scope. Complex productions may take longer.

---

## Submit a Video Request

[Submit Video Production Request](https://www.cognitoforms.com){:target="_blank" .action_btn}

---

## What to Include

- Project name and ministry/department
- Type of video (promo, recap, interview, etc.)
- Intended platform (sermon, social, website, screens)
- Deadline or event date
- Key message or talking points
- Any footage, photos, or assets you can provide
- On-camera participants (if applicable)

---

## Tips for a Smooth Process

- Book filming dates as early as possible — production team availability is limited
- Have your talking points or script outline ready before the shoot
- All video content must be approved by ministry leadership before submission

---

## Questions?

Reach out to the Creative team for availability or to scope your project before submitting a request.
