Use this form to request photography coverage from the OSC Creative team. This includes event photography, headshots, ministry highlights, and other photo needs.

> **Book early:** Photography requests should be submitted at least **2 weeks** in advance. Same-week requests may not be available.

---

## Submit a Photography Request

[Submit Photography Request](https://www.cognitoforms.com){:target="_blank" .action_btn}

---

## What to Include

- Event or project name and ministry/department
- Date, time, and location
- Type of photography needed (event, portrait, product, etc.)
- Estimated number of people or subjects
- Intended use (social media, print, web, internal)
- Any specific shots or moments that must be captured

---

## After the Shoot

- Edited photos are typically delivered within **5–7 business days**
- Photos will be shared via Google Drive or the approved file sharing platform
- All photos remain property of Our Savior's Church and must be used in accordance with media guidelines

---

## Questions?

Contact the Creative team to confirm availability or discuss your photography needs.
