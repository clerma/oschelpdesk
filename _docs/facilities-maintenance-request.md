Use the form below to submit a maintenance or facility repair request for your campus. Requests are reviewed by the Facilities team and prioritized based on urgency.

> **Urgent issues** (safety hazards, water leaks, HVAC failures) should be reported directly to your campus facilities contact by phone — do not wait for a form response.

---

## Submit a Maintenance Request

Select your campus:

- [Lafayette — Facility Maintenance Request](https://www.cognitoforms.com){:target="_blank"} *(Cognito Forms)*
- [Midtown — Maintenance Request](https://connect.ccbchurch.com){:target="_blank"} *(CCB Church)*

---

## What to Include

Provide as much detail as possible to help the team respond quickly:

- Location (building, room, or area)
- Description of the issue
- How long the issue has been present
- Photos if available

---

## Questions?

Contact your campus Facilities coordinator for follow-up on submitted requests.
