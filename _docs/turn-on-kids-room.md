This article explains how to properly **turn the Kids room ON and OFF** using the tablet, power rack, and sound board.
Please follow the steps in order to prevent equipment issues.

---

## ROOM ON (Starting the Room)

### 1. Turn the Room On Using the Tablet
- On the **small black mini tablet** (located to the **left of the monitor**)
- Enter code **407**
- Press the **green check button**
- Select **“Room On”**

---

### 2. Turn On the Power Rack
- Under the desk is a rack with power switches
- Turn **ALL switches ON**
- Go **left to right**

---

### 3. Turn On the Stage Screens (If Needed)
- If the stage screens do not power on automatically:
  - Use the **“Sharp” remote** located in the desk drawer
  - Turn the screens on manually

---

### 4. Set the Sound Board
- On the sound board:
  - Press the **“Pastor” button** (located on the right side)
  - This will show:
    - Handheld microphones
    - Computer channels
- **Make sure channels are unmuted**
  - The **red button below each channel** should not be active

---

## ROOM OFF (Shutting the Room Down)

### 1. Start Shutdown on the Sound Board
- On the sound board:
  - Scroll **up to the top left**
  - Press **File** (using the grey button)

---

### 2. Shut Down the Sound Board
- Scroll down to **Preferences**
- Move to the **right-side menu**
- Click **Shut Down**
- Wait until the board instructs you to power off

---

### 3. Turn Off the Power Rack
- Under the desk:
  - Flip power switches **right to left**
  - **Leave switches 8 and 10 ON**

---

### 4. Turn the Room Off Using the Tablet
- On the **small black mini tablet** (left of the monitor)
- Enter code **407**
- Press **“Room Off”**

---

## Important Notes
- Always follow the steps in order
- Do not skip steps
- If something doesn’t seem right, ask a **Team Lead** for help

Thank you for serving in Kids Ministry!
